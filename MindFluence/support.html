How to Use MindFluence

🧠 1. Starting a Training Session

Step 1 — Open the app and tap the Training Center tab at the bottom.
Step 2 — Choose one of the 5 cognitive domains: Memory, Logic, Focus, Creativity, or Decision.
Step 3 — Select your difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard).
Step 4 — Read the specific instructions for the exercise, then tap Start Exercise.
💡 Tip: Exercises are randomized! Each time you select a category, you'll get a different type of challenge to keep your brain guessing.

⚙️ 2. Auto-Advance Mode

Want a more immersive, high-pressure continuous training experience?

  1. Go to the Settings tab.
  2. Find the Training Preferences section.
  3. Toggle on Auto-Advance Mode.
  4. When enabled, timers will automatically submit your answers and move to the next question, reducing the need for manual tapping.

📈 3. Tracking Your Progress

Monitor your cognitive improvement over time:

  • Radar Chart — See your balanced strengths across all 5 domains on the Home screen.
  • Statistics Cards — Track your total sessions, current day streak, and total minutes trained today.
  • Achievements — Unlock medals by reaching specific milestones (e.g., 100 sessions, 7-day streak). View them in the Progress tab.

🎯 4. The 15-Minute Daily Plan

Consistency is key to cognitive training. On the Home screen, you will see a green progress ring indicating your Today's Training Plan.

Your goal is to complete 15 minutes of training per day. The ring will fill up automatically as you complete exercises across any category.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an internet connection to use MindFluence?

No. MindFluence is completely functional offline. All of your data, progress, and our extensive built-in question bank are stored entirely on your device.

Where is my data stored?

Your privacy is our priority. All training history, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or save your personal data to any external servers.

The questions are repeating, how do I get new ones?

MindFluence comes with a large built-in question bank that is constantly expanding. The system randomizes questions, but if you play heavily, you might see repeats. We periodically update the question bank in app updates to keep the content fresh.

How is the Radar Chart calculated?

Your radar chart (Memory, Logic, Focus, Creativity, Decision) is calculated based on your accumulated correct answers and scores within each specific training category. The more you train a specific area successfully, the further out that point on the radar will extend.

The app crashed or isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try force-quitting and relaunching the app. Ensure you are on the latest iOS version. If issues persist, please email us at daibou007@gmail.com with details about the problem and your device model.
