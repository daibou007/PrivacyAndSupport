Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

Summary: MindFluence is a fully offline, privacy-first cognitive training app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your training data, progress, and settings stay securely on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MindFluence ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. MindFluence is designed to work entirely on your device — no account, no server, no tracking.

By using MindFluence, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

MindFluence does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or track your usage behavior.
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs.

All training sessions, statistics, streak counts, and settings are stored locally on your device using iOS system storage (such as UserDefaults or Core Data) and are never transmitted anywhere.

3. Permissions

MindFluence does not request any sensitive permissions such as Location, Camera, Microphone, or Contacts. We respect your privacy by never asking for access to data that isn't absolutely necessary for the app to function.

4. Data Storage and Encryption

All data generated by the App (including your cognitive performance scores, medals, and preferences) is stored exclusively on your device. It is protected by iOS's built-in Data Protection (encryption at rest). If you back up your device to iCloud, this data may be included in the backup and is end-to-end encrypted by Apple's infrastructure.

Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored training data unless it is restored from an iCloud backup.

5. Third-Party Services

MindFluence does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or social login services. No data is shared with any third party under any circumstances.

6. Children's Privacy

MindFluence does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe and beneficial for users of all ages.

7. Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our end. All your data lives on your device and you maintain complete control over it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
