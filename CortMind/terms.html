3. Description of Service

Cort Mind is a digital thinking tool that implements the CoRT (Cognitive Research Trust) methodology developed by Dr. Edward de Bono. The App provides six structured thinking frameworks to help users organize their thoughts, make better decisions, and solve problems systematically:

  • PMI (Plus, Minus, Interesting)
  • CAF (Consider All Factors)
  • C&S (Consequences & Sequel)
  • AGO (Aims, Goals, Objectives)
  • APC (Alternatives, Possibilities, Choices)
  • Action Planning

The App supports handwritten note-taking via Apple Pencil and offers optional AI-powered analysis using Anthropic's Claude AI (requires separate API key).
