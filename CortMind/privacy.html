Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Summary: Cort Mind is a privacy-first thinking tools app. Your thinking sessions and notes stay on your device. AI analysis is optional and only uses your personal Anthropic API key when you choose to enable it.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Cort Mind ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. Cort Mind is designed to be an offline-first productivity tool that helps you think more clearly using structured thinking frameworks.

By using Cort Mind, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Cort Mind does not collect any personal information by default. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or track your usage behavior.
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs.
  • We do not collect location data or device sensors.

All your thinking sessions, handwritten notes, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS system storage and are never transmitted anywhere without your explicit action.

3. Photo Library Permission

Cort Mind requests access to your photo library only when you explicitly choose to save handwritten notes or diagrams as images. This permission is used solely to save exported content to your photo library - we never read, scan, or upload any existing photos from your library.

You can revoke photo library permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Cort Mind.

4. Optional AI Analysis

Cort Mind offers optional AI-powered analysis of your thinking sessions using Anthropic's Claude AI. This feature works as follows:

  • You provide your own Anthropic API key - we never supply or manage API keys
  • API keys are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device only
  • Your thinking content is sent to Anthropic only when you tap "AI Analysis" and only with your API key
  • We do not store or transmit your API key - it goes directly from your device to Anthropic's servers
  • AI analysis is completely optional - the app works fully offline without it

When you use AI analysis, your thinking content and API key are transmitted to Anthropic's servers according to Anthropic's privacy policy and terms of service. We do not intercept, store, or have access to this data.

5. Data Storage

All data generated by the App is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Thinking sessions and notes - stored in iOS Core Data and document files
  • App preferences and settings - stored in iOS UserDefaults
  • API keys - stored securely in iOS Keychain
  • Export files - saved to your chosen location (Files app, Photos, etc.)

Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data except for any files you explicitly exported to other locations.

6. Third-Party Services

Cort Mind integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic API - for optional AI analysis (only when you provide an API key and explicitly request analysis)
  • iOS Photos Framework - for saving exported images to your photo library (only when you explicitly choose to save)

We do not integrate any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or social services. No data is shared with any third party except as described above for AI analysis.

7. Children's Privacy

Cort Mind does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for all ages and can help develop critical thinking skills.

8. Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data by default, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our end. All your data lives on your device and can be removed by:

  • Uninstalling the App (removes all sessions and settings)
  • Deleting individual sessions within the app
  • Manually deleting exported files from your photo library or files
