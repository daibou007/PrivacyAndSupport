Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

Summary: TintSnap is a fully offline, privacy-first app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how TintSnap ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. TintSnap is designed to work entirely on your device — no account, no server, no tracking.

By using TintSnap, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

TintSnap does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or track your usage behavior.
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs.

All color samples, palettes, and history you create are stored locally on your device using iOS system storage (UserDefaults / local files) and are never transmitted anywhere.

3. Camera Permission

TintSnap requires access to your device camera solely to sample colors from the real world in real time. Camera frames are processed locally and are never saved, uploaded, or transmitted to any server. You can revoke camera permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera.

4. Data Storage

All data generated by the App (color history, saved palettes, preferences) is stored exclusively on your device. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data.

5. Third-Party Services

TintSnap does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or social login services. No data is shared with any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

TintSnap does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for all ages.

7. Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our end. All your data lives on your device and can be removed simply by uninstalling the App.

If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at daibou007@gmail.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
